From Malaysian Founder to 2025 Gambling Debt Court Case

## Lead
NagaWorld, the largest casino in the Mekong region, is not just Cambodia's premier Integrated Resort destination‚Äîit also shares deep connections with Malaysia in terms of founding, funding, and recent legal proceedings.

## Background
NagaWorld was established in 1995 on prime land just one mile from Cambodia's National Palace, funded by Malaysian investors. This marked the beginning of the business relationship between the two countries in the casino industry.

## Main Content

### Malaysian Founder
**Dr. Chen Lip Keong**, founder and former CEO of NagaCorp, was born in Malaysia and trained as a physician before finding success in property development. He came to Cambodia in the early 1990s looking for oil but discovered opportunities in the gaming industry instead.

Dr. Chen passed away in December 2023, but his legacy lives on‚ÄîNagaWorld has become the largest Integrated Resort in the Mekong region.

### Signing Ceremony in Kuala Lumpur
On January 2, 1995, the Royal Government of Cambodia and Ariston signed the **Sihanoukville Development Agreement** in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The ceremony was attended by Dr. Chen and the former Prime Ministers of both countries.

### Malaysia Gambling Debt Court Case (2025)
In March 2025, the **Malaysian Federal Court** ruled on a significant case related to NagaWorld, confirming that **Dato' Ting Ching Lee** has no legal obligation to repay gambling debts from his trip to NagaWorld in Cambodia.

**Case Details:**
- Originated from a gambling trip in January 2015
- Organized by Ting Siu Hua, a tour agent and junket operator under Huang Group
- Dato' Ting was granted credit lines totaling $1.5 million and a rolling rebate of $193,800
- Court ruled that overseas gambling debts are not enforceable in Malaysia
